## Full description

Start each day with ancient wisdom.

FieldSong delivers a daily Bhagavad Gita verse paired with a Stoic philosophy quote — two traditions, one insight. Each verse comes with a modern interpretation, a concrete action step, and a reflection prompt to help you build a clarity practice that fits your morning.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Choose your intention — clarity, courage, patience, acceptance, discipline, or perspective
2. Receive a daily verse matched to your intention
3. Read the Stoic parallel — Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, or Epictetus
4. Reflect and journal your thoughts

FEATURES
- Daily ritual: curated Gita verse, modern interpretation, Stoic parallel, and action step
- Journal: save reflections and revisit your practice over time
- Bookmarks: save verses that resonate
- Email delivery: your verse arrives by email too — reply to save a journal entry
- Dark-only UI designed for calm, focused reading

FIELDSONG+ (optional upgrade)
- Unlimited journal history and search
- Unlimited bookmarks
- Theme journeys: 7-day guided sequences
- Cross-tradition deep-dives

FieldSong is for people who value wisdom from multiple traditions. Spiritual but not religious. Curious, not dogmatic. If you want a simple daily practice grounded in the world's oldest philosophy — this is it.
